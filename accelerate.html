The Client

Donna is a fictional client who is head of marketing at a firm called Accelerate. Donna was seeking a developer to fix the company's WordPress site.

The Problem

Donna needed a developer to create a child theme, custom posts and templates, custom fields, a contact form, social share buttons in the footer, and add a twittter module to the homepage.

My Role

Accelerate was an assignment I completed for my Skillcrush Front End Web Developer course to navigate the ins and outs of working with a freelance client. Donna would email me each day with a list of what she needed done. I cloned her site from GitHub, created a custom theme and began to custom code the updates she needed from her static photoshop designs.
