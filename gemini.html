The Project

Project Gemini is a website that two classmates and I created. We were tasked with creating a fictional business. Project Gemini - inspired by Virgin Galactic - is a company making space travel more accessible to individual people.

The Problem

We had fun with our idea and created a shared style that would feature space imagery and neon green to give it a space age design aesthetic. We designed a logo that would represent our playful business idea. We built of each other's work using GitHub to create a cohesive site.

My Role

I designed our "Testimonials" page. It features two reviews from happy customers, Beyonce and Justen Bieber, in which they describe their adventures in space.
