The Client

Jill Lindsey is an artisinal clothing and homegoods store, wine and coffee bar, wellness center, and event space. The store serves as a community hub, inviting customers and neighbors alike to spend time in the space and experience something new.

The Problem

The owner, Jill Lindsey, wanted to update her website to showcase all of the different aspects of her business.

My Role

For an assignment for my NYCDA Front End 101 course, I created a mock-website using Bootstrap that demonstrates the store's diverse offerings. I used a one-page scroll format so that the viewer can easily discover that there is also a cafe and that there are daily events, etc. I utilized a modal to show the cafe menu and another to show the information about renting the space as a venue. I used a zoom function to enlarge the event calendar for easier reading. This website was used as a model when I re-designed the actual Jill lindsey website on the e-commerce shopify platform.
