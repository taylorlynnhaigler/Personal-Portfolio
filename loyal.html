The Client

Loyal is a Brooklyn based dog walking and overnight care service. Their mission is to provide top quality dog care to help busy pet owners.

The Problem

The owner inherited the business from a good friend who was ready to pass it on. He wanted to uphold the pillars of the previous business, personalized care and strong customer service, but wanted to re-brand to make it his own company.

My Role

I was commissioned to help with rebranding Loyal, formerly known Sid & Tapka's. I created a new logo using Adobe Indesign and created a Wordpress site. The owner can easily access the site to list their services and field inquiries from his evergrowing customer base.
